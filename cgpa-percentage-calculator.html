Supported Formulas

Different institutions use different conversion formulas. Select the one that matches your university's grading system.

CBSE / Standard
CGPA × 9.5
Most common in India. A CGPA of 10 equals approximately 95%.
AICTE India
(CGPA − 0.75) × 10
Used by engineering, pharmacy and MBA programmes in India.
US 4.0 Scale
(CGPA ÷ 4.0) × 100
Standard in the United States, Canada and many global institutions.
University (10-pt)
CGPA × 10
Used by some universities that treat 10.0 as equivalent to 100%.
5-Point Scale
CGPA × 20
Used in some Nigerian and West African university systems.
